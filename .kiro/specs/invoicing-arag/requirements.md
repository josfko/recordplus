# Requirements Document

## Introduction

Invoicing module for ARAG insurance cases and mileage expenses. Handles fixed-fee invoice generation (203€ + 21% IVA), mileage expense calculations by judicial district, and the complete invoice workflow including PDF generation, signing, and email sending.

## Status: PENDING

This spec will be developed after email-integration is approved.

## Planned Requirements

1. ARAG invoice generation (fixed 203€ + 21% IVA)
2. Mileage expense calculation by partido judicial
3. Configurable mileage rate table
4. Invoice workflow automation (generate → sign → email)
5. Invoice history and status tracking
